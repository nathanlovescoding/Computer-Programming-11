Tips & Tricks

Become a better skiier


Carving

Carving refers to a technique where you move down the slope in parallel, S-shaped turns by having your skis on edge throughout the turn, leaving clean, arc-shaped tracks in the snow.


Pole Planting

Pole Planting refers to a controlled movement where the downhill ski pole is briefly planted into the snow, typically at the edge change of a turn, to help with balance, timing, and initiating a turn.


Hockey Stop

A Hocket Stop is a quick, controlled method of stopping by rapidly turning your skis sideways across the slope and setting the edges to create friction and stop, similar to how hockey players stop on ice.
